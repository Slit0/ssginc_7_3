  # Spring Boot 2.X
	 <dependency>
	    <groupId>org.springdoc</groupId>
	    <artifactId>springdoc-openapi-ui</artifactId>
	    <version>1.8.0</version>
	</dependency>

      의존성 설정후  http://localhost:8090/app/swagger-ui/index.html 요청하면 됨.
